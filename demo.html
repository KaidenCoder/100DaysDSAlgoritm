\n\nChingthakta tambiba \n\n1 Amahiksung miyamsing adu uraduna Ibungona chingda lengkhatle, amasung phamtharaba matamda Ibungogi seboksingna mangonda laklammi, \n\n2 Amasung Ibungona ya kathoktuna makhoida tambiba houre, \n\nYaiphabasing (Haraobasing) \n\n3 Thawaida tonbasing adu yaiphabani, swaiga leibak adu makhoigini. \n\n4 Tengtha nairibasing adu yaiphabani, maramdi makhoibu nungngaihanbigani. \n\n5 Pukning ,tappasing adu yaiphabani, maramdi prithibi asi makhoina phangpham thoki. \n\n6 Dharma chenbagidamak aram khouranglibasing adu yaiphabani, maramdi makhoibu thalhanbigani. \n\n7 Nungsiheibasing adu yaiphabani maramdi makhoina nungsibiba phanggani. \n\n8 Pukning asengbasing adu yaiphabani, maramdi makhoina Iswarbu ujagani. \n\n9 Tapthaba pibasing adu yaiphabani, maramdi makhoibu Iswargi machasing kougani. \n\n10 Dharmachenbagidamak awaba phanglibasing adu yaiphabani, maramdi swarga leibak makhoigini. \n\n11 Eigi maramgi damak misingna nakhoibu cheiraba amasung otneiraba, aduga nakhoigi nathakta phattaba pumnamak theijillabada nakhoi yaiphabani. \n\n12 Haraojou amasung thoidokna nungaijou, maramdi swargada nakhoigi mana chaoi, maramdi nakhoigi namangda leiramba Iswargi wa takthokpasingbu asumna ot neikhi. \n\nThum amasung mangan \n\n13 Nakhoidi taibangpanbagi thumni, adubu thum aduna magi mahao mangkhrabadi, madu karamna haohangadage? Adudagidi madu hundoktuna misinggi khongna netpa nattana karisu kannadre. \n\n14 Nakhoidi taibangpanbagi manganni, chingthakta leiba sahar lotpa jade. \n\n15 Aduga misingna thaomei thallaga thummuk makhada thamde, toigumbasung thaomei makhong mathakta thamduna imungda leiba pumnamakpu ngalhalli. \n\n16 Matou asumna misingna nakhoigi aphaba thabak uduna swargada nakhoigi Napabu thagatnanaba nakhoigi mangal adu misinggi mamangda ngansanu. \n\nWayel yathang ariba amasung Jisugi wayel yathang \n\n17 Eina wayel yathang adu nattraga Iswargi wa takthokpasinggi wa adu manghange lakpani haina ningganu, eidi manghanba lakpa natte, touigumbasung thunghanba lakpani. \n\n18 Maramdi eina nakhoida thanggeisengna hairibani, swarga amasung prithibi mangdriba phaoba wayel yathang adudagi leii machet ama nattraga bindu amatasu, pumnamak thungdriba phaoba, mangloi. \n\n19 Maram aduna yathangsing asidagi khwaidagi pikpa ama thugaiduna misingda tambirabadi, swarga leibakta mahakpu khwaidagi pikpa kougani, touigumbasung kanagumbana yathangsing ngaktuna misingda tambirabadi, mahakpu swarga leibakta achouba kougani. \n\n20 Eina nakhoida hairibani, nakhoigi dharma chenbana wayen yathangi ojhasing amasung Pharisisinggi dharmachenbadagi hendrabadi, nakhoina swarga leibakta keidoungaidasu changloi. \n\nMihatpana awabada taba/b> \n\n21 Nangna mi hatpharoi, amasung kanagumbana mi hatlurabadi, wayengi awabada tagani, haibasi thainagi makhoi aduda haikhibadu nakhoina tare. \n\n22 Touigumbasung eina nakhoida hairi, kanagumbana magi machin manaobu saorabadi, wayengi awabada tagani, amasung kanagumbana machin manaobu raka haiba di, mahakna ahallupki awabada tagani, amasung kanagumbana apangba amangba nang hairabadi, mahak norokki meigi awabada tagani. \n\n23 Maram aduna nangna latna pungda nanggi katnaba pot puraklabada nanggi nachin nanaona nanggi nathakta karigumba ama leikhibadu ningsinglaklabadi, \n\n24 Katnnba, adu latnapunggi mamang aduda thanamduna, chatlaga nanggi nachin nanaoga hanna channaru, amasung madudagi laktuna nanggi katnaba pot adu katcharo. \n\n25 Nangga wa thoknariba aduna nangga loinana lambida leiringeida maga, thuna chamnou, adu nattrabadi wa thoknariba aduna nangbu wayenba mapuda pithokpa yai, amasung wayenba mapuna nangbu phamnaibada sinnaduna nangbu keisumsangda thajinba yai. \n\n26 Eina nangonda thanggei sengna hairibani, nangna singgadaba aroiba peisa adu singdriha makei mapham adudagi khak thoklakpa yaroi. \n\n \n\n27 Nangna migi nupi lannapharoi, haikhiba asi nakhoina tare, \n\n28 Touigumbasung eina nakhoida hairi, nupi amabu apambana sumhattuna yengba mi khuding makna magi pukningda mahakka loinana lannaba thabak toukhrabani. \n\n29 Amasung nanggi yethangba namitna nangbu papta khudi thihallabadi, khottoktuna nangondagi hundoku, maramdi nasa apumba norokta tahanbadagi nanggi hakchanggi kayat ama mangkhibana nanggi kannabani. \n\n30 Amasung nanggi yetthangba nakhutna nangbu papta khudi thihallabadi, kakthattuna nangondagi hundoku, maramdi nasa apumba norokta tabadagi nanggi hakchanggi kayat ama mangkhibana nanggi kannabani. \n\n31 Asumnasu haikhre, kanagumbana magi nupi khainage hairabadi, khainabagi lairik nupi aduda pisanu. \n\n32 Touigumbasung eina nakhoida hairi, lannaba thabakki nattana nupi khainaba mi khudingmakna mabu mawa lannabi oihallabani amasung kanagumbana khainarabi mahakpu luhonglabadi, nupi lannaba thabak toui. \n\n33 Amukka, thainagi makhoi aduda haikhiba adu nakhoina tare, madudi, nangna lanna wasakkanu, adubu nanggi wasakpa adu prabhuda pangthoku. \n\n34 Touigumbasung eina nakhoida hairi, khak wasakpharoi, swarga pandunasu sakkanu, maramdi Iswargi phambanni, \n\n35 Leipak pandunasu sakkanu, maramdi Ibungogi khonggi khutnetni, aduga Jerusalem pandunasu sakkanu, maramdi achouba ningthougi saharani. \n\n36 Nanggi nakok paidunasu sakkanu, maramdi nangna sam mana amatasu ngouhange nattraga muhange ngamba natte. \n\n37 Touigumbasung nanggi wa adu hoi hairaga, hoi haiyu, mai hairaga, mai haiyu, amasung asidagi henba makheidi phattabagi mapudagini. \n\n38 Mit amagi mahut mit ama, ya amagi mahut ya ama, haikhiba asi nakhoina tare. Adubu eina nakhoida hairi, phattraba adubu thingganu. \n\n39 Touigumbasung kanagumbana nanggi yetthangba nakhajaida khupakna tharabadi, mangonda atoppa adusu onsillu. \n\n40 Amasung nangbu wayensangda pukhattuna nanggi phurit louningba mahak aduda nanggi innaphi adusu pithokho. \n\n41 Amasung kanagumbana nangbu mail ama chathaba namlabadi, magi mail ani chatminnou. \n\n42 Nangonda nijaraba mahak aduda pinou, amasung nangondagi pujaningba mahak adudagi leithokkanu. \n\n43 Nanggi keiroi leikaibu mingsiyu amasung nanggi yeknababu tukkachou, haikhiba asi nakhoina tare. \n\n44 Adubu eina nakhoida hairi, nakhoina swargada leiba nakhoigi napagi machasing oiraknanaba yeknabasingbu nungsiyu, amasung nakhoibu otneibasinggi damak haijabiyu, \n\n45 Maramdi Ibungo mahakna phattaba amasung aphabasinggi mathakta Ibungo mahakki numit thokhalli, aduga dharma chenba amasung chendabasinggi mathakta nong chuhalli. \n\n46 Maramdi nakhoibu nungsibasingbu nakhoina nungsirabadi, nakhoigi kari mana leibage? Khajna thoubasingnasu adum touba nattra? \n\n47 Atnasung nakhoina nakhoigi nachin nanaosingdata khurumlabadi ateisingdagi nakhoina kari henna toubage? Atoppa jatsingnasu adum touba nattra? \n\n48 Maram asina nakhoigi swargagi napana mapung phabagumna nakhoisu mapung phagadabani.
