\n\nMisingbu Wayendanaba Tambiba \n\n1 Nakhoibu wayenkhidanaba wayelluganu, \n\n2 Maramdi nakhoina yelluriba wayel aduna nakhoibu wayengani, amasung nakhoina olluriba chang aduna nak-hoidasu ollagani, \n\n3 Aduga nangmakki namitta khangel leiba adu khandaduna, nanggi nachin nanaogi mamitta thunamba leiba adu karigi yenglibano? \n\n4 Aduga karamna nachin nanaoda, namittagi thunamba adu louthokpige, haigadage? amasung yengu, nanggi namitta khangel leiri, \n\n5 Aphasajaningba, nangna ahanbada namittagi khangel adu louthokkho, aduga matung aduda nangna nanggi nachin nanaogi mamittagi thunamba adu louthokpa sengna ugani, \n\n6 Asengba pot adu huisingda piganu, aduga nakhoigi mukta adu okki mamangda hunjinganu, Kanana khanggani, makhoina, madubu makhongna netsillaga onsillaktuna nakhoibu hogaiba yai, \n\nHaijanaba Thoujinba \n\n7 Nijou, nakhoida pibigani, thijou, nakhoina phangjagani, amasung thong yeijou, nakhoida hangdok-pigani, \n\n8, Maramdi nijaba mi khudingmakna phangi, thijaba aduna khangi, amasung yeijaba aduda hangdokpigani, \n\n9 Nakhoigi naraktagi kanana mahakki macha nipana tal niraga nung mangonda pigadage, \n\n10 Nattraga nga niraga lil mangonda pigadage? \n\n11 Maram aduna nakhoina phattaba oidunasu nakhoigi nachasingda aphaba khudon piba heirabadi, nakhoigi swargada leiba napana nijaba makhoida kayamuk thoidok hendokna aphaba pot pibigaba! \n\n12 Maram aduna nakhoina misingbu nakhoida touhanningba pumnamak adu nakhoinasu makhoida tou, maramdi masina wayel yathang adugi amasung Iswargi wa takthokpasinggi lairikki machini, \n\nLambi Ani \n\n13 Nakhoina akhuba thong aduda changu, maramdi amangbada tanaba thong adu lao-i, aduga lambi adu pak-i, amasung maduda changba mi yammi, \n\n14 Maramdi hingnabada taba thong adu khui, aduga lambi adu chappi, amasung madu phangba mi yamde, \n\nWahei amasung Thabak \n\n15 Yaogi phijet setpa, adubu manungdadi charamnaba keisal oina nakhoida lakpa Iswargi wa takthoksinnabasingbu cheksillu, \n\n16 Nakhoina makhoigi maheidagi makhoibu khanggani, Misingna tingkhang pambidigi draksha mahei, aduga tingkhang pambidigi hayit heknabra? \n\n17 Matou asumna aphaba upambi khudingmakna aphaba mahei palli, touigumbasung phattaba upambina phattaba mahei palli, \n\n18 Aphaba upambina phattaba mahei panba, aduga phattaba upambina aphaba mahei panba ngamde, \n\n19 Aphaba mahei pandaba upambi khudingmak adubu yanthektuna meinungda langsilli, \n\n20 Maram asina makhoigi maheidagi nakhoina makhoibu khanggani, \n\n21 Eingonda prabhu, prabhu, haiba mi khudingmakna swarga leibakta changloi, touigumbasung swargada leiba ipagi ningba towriba mi aduna chang-gani, \n\n22 Numit aduda ayambana eingonda haigani, prabhu, prabhu, eikhoina Ibungoogi minggi mapanna Iswargi wa tak-thok chakhidra, Ibungoogi minggi mapanna bhutsingbu tanthokchakhidra, amasung Ibungoogi minggi mapanna toungamdaba thabak ayamba toujakhidra? \n\n23 Matam aduda eina makhoida phongna haidokkani, eina keidoungeidasu nakhoibu khanglamde, he touheidaba toubasa, eingondagi chatthokkhro, \n\n24 Maram asina eigi wasing asi taraga touba mi khudingmakpu nungjao mathakta yum saba asingba mi amaga chang-damnagani, \n\n25 Amasung nong kanna taraktuna ising ichao lakle, nungsit sitle, amasung yum aduda yeirei, adubu nung-jao adugi mathakta houba mamma tuk-hide, \n\n26 Amasung eigi wasing asi taraga toudaba mi khudingmakpu leingoi mathakta yum saba apangba mi amaga changdamnagani, \n\n27 Amasung nong kanna taraktuna ichao lakle, nungsit side, amasung yum aduda phujinduna madu tukhi, amasung madu matu thina tukhi, \n\n28 Amasung asum thokkhi, Jisuna wasing asi loirabada, miyam aduna Ibungoogi tambiba aduda ingak ngaklammi, \n\n29 Maramdi makhoigi sastragi ojhasinggumna nattana matik leiba migumna tambirammi,
